Welcome!

I'm Dallin Clark, a graphics programmer focused on rendering and animation pipelines. I'm currently an undergraduate at BYU and an assistant researcher in the BYU Advanced Graphics Lab. I also work as the Pipeline TD for the short film Honey Business.

I love everything related to animation software development, and I get especially excited about low-level graphics. In my free time, I enjoy building side projects with OpenGL and Vulkan implementing different SIGGRAPH papers, and learning more about the state of the art in graphics. I would love to pursue these topics further in my career.
